Full-house steakhouse profile: dine-in checks, takeout, Uber Eats,
DoorDash. The public Maven Analytics 'Pizza Place Sales' dataset
(21k real full-service orders) supplies only the within-day hour
rhythm and order-value distribution shape, both generic across
full-service restaurants. Day-of-week rhythm, month-to-month
seasonality, and occasion effects are hand-built for a steakhouse,
not read from the pizza data, since those do not transfer: this
profile is dinner-dominant with Fri/Sat peaks, spikes on Valentine's/
Mother's/Father's Day and NYE, and a deliberate DOWN day on Super
Bowl Sunday (a peak day for pizza, a slow night for steak service).
Modulated by real Knoxville, TN weather (rain lifts delivery, dents
walk-ins) and TN holidays.
Replace these files with real POS / Uber Eats Manager / DoorDash
Merchant Portal exports; the pipeline runs unchanged.
